This website will briefly introduce Stephen Chow

Chow Sing-chi (Chinese: 周星馳, born 22 June 1962) is a Hong Kong filmmaker, former actor and comedian, known for Shaolin Soccer and Kung Fu Hustle. Fans and media have dubbed him the "King of Comedy", and he himself has been used as a synonym for "comedy" and "nonsense", with far-reaching influence on Internet culture.
